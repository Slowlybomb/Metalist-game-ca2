Lore

You're a die-hard fan of the football team "Metalist". and never miss their games. You have traveled to Donetsk city to catch their match against"Shakhtar". However, you also with a strong civil position about drinking in public (you love to drink in public), so if the police spot you, they'll likely approach you (leading to jail time). That is why you have to avoid contact with them. Additionally, the local ultras are notorious for their hostility towards visitors, and if they catch sight of you, they won't hesitate to attack without warning. Hence, you need to evade them at all costs. The your aim is to prepare your self to football match. You need to take, ticket, scarf with your favorite team emblem and flare. Do not miss the game!

How to play

Use WASD to controll your player.

You also can hide in trash bins, just came close to them and press "SPACE"! If enemy see how you get in trash bin, he confuses and stops following you.

Mechanics

Enemies does not see you behind the wall. They have limited vision radius. If you will not be seen, you will get extra points to your final result.

Enemies

Here is two types of enemies:

  • Chaser

    He is staying at one place . Then he starts chasing you.

  • Patroller

    He is patrolling territory until he will see you. Then he starts chasing you, but after he lost you, he starts patrolling again.
